App Prototyping using Figma

This is a wireframe or prototype project that my group and I made for a design class.

At my university, we have a bus system that goes around campus. However, since many people pass by the campus and with unpredictable Wisconsin weather, many buses would be delayed and overall inconsistent.

We chose to create an app that displays bus locations and provides users with timely alerts about delays and schedule updates.
