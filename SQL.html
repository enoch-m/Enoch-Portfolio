This project simulates the operations of a restaurant by organizing data about customers, chefs, dishes, dining tables, orders, and reviews. I designed a relational database that supports everything from placing and tracking food orders to analyzing customer preferences and dish performance.

I focused on building a system that reflects how a real restaurant might manage its day-to-day activities such as assigning chefs to specific dishes, logging special customer requests, keeping track of total sales, and collecting feedback through reviews.

Some features include:

  • Database structure creation through tables
  • Sample data population
  • Use of aggregation to provide business insights

This project, I focused on highlighting clear data structure organization so that information can be linked to one another seamlessly, making it easy to navigate.
