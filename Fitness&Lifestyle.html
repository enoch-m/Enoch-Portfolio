Fitness and Lifestyle HTML/CSS Project

Using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, my team and I built a fully functional website aimed at helping users improve their lifestyle and fitness habits. We designed an intuitive user interface, implemented interactive features like dynamic goal tracking and personalized tips, and ensured responsive design across devices. From brainstorming ideas to creating prototypes to implementing code, my team and I worked together to form this tool.

I personally wanted to focus on user-centered design and what we are trying to communicate to users. I prioritized clear layout, accessible navigation, and consistent visual language to ensure users felt encouraged and informed as they engaged with the site. Please feel free to take a look with the provided application!
